What are cookies?

Cookies are small text fragments stored on your device by a website or app. They are read on subsequent visits to recognize your browser and apply saved settings. Some last only for a session, while others persist for a defined period. Similar technologies such as local storage, pixels, and SDK identifiers work in comparable ways. In this policy, we refer to them collectively as “cookies” used by play jonny and partners operating on playjonny.
